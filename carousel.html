Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions? We Have Answers!

Where do you source your beans?

We work directly with trusted coffee growers and specialty importers from some of the world's most respected coffee-producing regions, including Central and South America, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Where is the shelf life of your loose tea?

For optimal flavour, we recommend consuming our teas within 6 months of purchase. Although they remain safe and aromatic for up to a year when stored in a tight container

Do you have wholesale opportunities?

Yes. We partner with cafés, restaurants, offices, retailers, and other businesses looking to serve or sell premium coffee. Wholesale partners can access competitive pricing, flexible ordering options, and ongoing support.

How is shipping handled for freshness?

Freshness is one of our top priorities. Coffee is roasted in small batches and shipped shortly after roasting whenever possible. Orders are carefully packaged to preserve flavour and aroma during transit.
